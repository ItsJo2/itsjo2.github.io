Drop Designs is a curated platform offering high-quality, completely free graphics and illustrations for designers, developers, creators, and everyone in between.

Privacy Policy

At Drop Designs, we respect your privacy. This document outlines how your data is handled on our platform.

What Data is Collected

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you when you browse, view, or download designs. We only gather anonymous and aggregated analytics (such as general page views and download count tracking) to optimize our service and see which designs are most popular.

Use of Third-Party Services

We use industry-standard external services to help power our platform:

  • Firebase: Used for database hosting, securely managing illustration records, and keeping download statistics.
  • Cloudinary: Used for secure hosting and lightning-fast delivery of illustration preview images and source files.
  • Affiliate Ad Partners: We feature advertisements from reputable hosts, security companies, and web partners to fund the site.

Cookies Policy

Drop Designs may use cookies and local storage to save basic visitor preferences (such as your UI view options) and to ensure our partner affiliate ads are tracked correctly. You can disable cookies inside your web browser settings if you wish, though some features may behave differently.

Questions & Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please feel free to reach out to us at: dropdesigns.contact@gmail.com
